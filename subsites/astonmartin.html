Aston Martin’s original Formula 1 foray – over half a century ago – lasted just five races.
This time, though, it’s serious. This F1 squad are no strangers to success,
having won in their original guise of Jordan and most recently as Racing Point in 2020.
Renowned for their ability to punch above their weight, and now with a four-time champion leading their driver line-up,
Aston Martin are very much a team to watch…
